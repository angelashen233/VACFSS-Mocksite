VACFSS is a community-driven organization where every role contributes to the well-being of Indigenous children, youth, and families in Vancouver.

Why Work With Us

A Place Where Work Has Meaning

At VACFSS, you are not just filling a position — you are part of a community committed to cultural safety, healing, and the future of Indigenous families.

Culturally Grounded

Indigenous culture and values are woven into everything we do — from case planning to staff ceremonies and cultural support.

Community Impact

Your work directly strengthens families and supports Indigenous children to grow up safe, connected, and proud of who they are.

Growth & Learning

We invest in our staff through ongoing training, supervision, professional development, and cultural learning opportunities.

Collaborative Team

Work alongside a dedicated, diverse team of social workers, cultural practitioners, administrators, and community advocates.

Application Process

How to Apply

Our hiring process is straightforward and respectful of your time. We are committed to creating a welcoming experience for all applicants.

1
Submit Your Application

Email your résumé and cover letter to hr@vacfss.com with the position title in the subject line.

2
Phone Screen

Our HR team will contact shortlisted candidates for a brief introductory call to discuss your background and the role.

3
Interview

Participate in a panel interview with program staff. We use strength-based, culturally informed interview practices.

4
References & Offer

We check references and conduct required background screenings before extending a formal offer of employment.
