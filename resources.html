A directory of trusted resources for children, youth, and families navigating housing, education, health, and more.

These resources are provided by external organizations and are not operated by VACFSS. Listings reflect those shared on the official VACFSS website and are provided for information purposes — please contact each organization directly for current details.

General Support

General Support

RCY BC

Supports youth and their families in dealing with the provincial child and youth welfare system.

Aged Out

Empowers youth in and from government care across BC with practical, peer-informed guidance.

Pacific Community Resources Society (PCRS)

Youth and family support, education, employment, and addiction counselling.

Vancouver Youth Portal

The City of Vancouver's youth information hub for local programs and services.

FRAFCA Prevention Services Guide

Aboriginal resources covering meals, shelters, treatment, housing, employment, and education.

Housing

Housing

Aged Out Housing

Information and resources for finding housing in BC.

Urban Native Youth Association (UNYA)

Housing options and transition programs for Indigenous youth.

Lu'ma Native Housing

Application process and housing information for Indigenous families.

Aboriginal Emergency Shelter

Hot meals and breakfast offered on a first-come basis.

BC Housing Assistance

Eligibility and application resources for provincial housing assistance.

Subsidized Housing Program

BC Housing's subsidized rental housing options.

Directions Safehouse

Secure housing for ages 16–18 with permanent housing support.

Vancouver Native Housing Society

Housing services for the urban Indigenous community.

Watari Transitioning to Independence Project (TIP)

Subsidy program for youth at risk of homelessness.

Education

Education

Aged Out Education

High school completion, student loans, fee waivers, and scholarships.

UNYA Education Programs

Native Youth Learning Centre and school support services.

PCRS Baristas Training

Employment training program delivered in collaboration with Starbucks.

Agreements with Young Adults (AYA)

Provincial program supporting young adults transitioning from care.

BladeRunners Program

Employment program overview, including eligibility and locations across BC.

Storwell Foster Children Bursary

Bursary application and funding for youth from foster care.

Youth Educational Assistance Fund

Financial support for former youth in care pursuing education.

Scholarships and Bursaries Guide

In-depth guidance for finding scholarships and bursaries.

Wellness

Mental Health, Addictions & Wellness

Aged Out Health and Wellness

Medical help, counselling, and stress management resources.

VCH Needle Exchange

Needle disposal and exchange services through Vancouver Coastal Health.

Supervised Injection Sites

Insite and Powell Street Gateway supervised consumption locations.

VCH Youth Health Services

Immunizations, mental health, and pregnancy services for youth.

UNYA Health Programs

Mediation program and clinical counselling services.

Lu'ma Medical Centre

Culturally integrated primary healthcare for the Indigenous community.

PCRS Aboriginal Youth Empowerment

Support for trauma and crime victims among Indigenous youth.

Astra Program

Assistance for youth changing their relationship with alcohol and drugs.

FBCYICN Mental Health Resources

Online and phone-based mental health support directory.

Qmunity

Support programs and counselling for LGBTQ2S+ folks.

Watari Counselling Services

Counselling and outreach support programs for youth and individuals.

Vancouver Urgent Primary Care

Same-day care for non-emergency medical conditions.

Surrey Mental Health Urgent Care

Urgent mental health and substance use support centre.

Aged Out Personal Life

Food Asset Maps

Low-cost and free food options across Vancouver and Richmond.

Free Clothing Resources

List of Vancouver organizations offering free clothing.

VCH Healthy Eating

Meal planning tools and nutrition videos.

UNYA Sports and Arts

Community connection through recreation and arts programming.

Lu'ma Aboriginal Youth Mentorship

Mentorship support for youth transitioning from foster care.

Friendship Centre Recreation

Pow Wow dancing, basketball, and soccer programs.

Fostering Change

Policy advocacy and events supporting youth aging out of care.

Vancity Financial Literacy

Advocates for youth in and from care, including national and international policy collaboration.
